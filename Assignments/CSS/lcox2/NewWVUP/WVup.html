Sherlock Holmes Comes to the WVUP Stage!

West Virginia University at Parkersburg presents Astral Theatre Collective’s live performance of “Sherlock Holmes and the Beast of Whitechapel.” The show takes place Saturday, Oct. 1 at 7 p.m. in the WVU Parkersburg College Theatre.

Free kidney screening event to take place at WVU Parkersburg Oct. 1

West Virginia University at Parkersburg is partnering with the National Kidney Foundation to host a kidney screening. The screening takes place at the WVU Parkersburg College Activities Center on Saturday, Oct. 1 from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. This kidney screening event is free.

Two employees recognized for years of service with state

Two West Virginia University at Parkersburg employees were recently recognized for their years of service with the state. At the September Board of Governors meeting, President Fletcher Lamkin presented Debbie Richards (40 years of service) and Jeff Olson (20 years of service) with certificates from Gov. Earl Ray Tomblin.
